Each of these computers is available for booking during open hours (see home page). Pricing is calculated within your session based upon the table below.

Customers wishing to use their own laptop are charged an additional £1.50 per session.

The maximum amount of time a customer can acccess the Internet service in a single session is six hours. If extra time is desired, it must be calculated as a new session.

For example, a customer using their own laptop for 95 minutes will need to buy from the fourth unit (Up to 120 minutes) and pay the connectivity fee of £1.50. A total of only £6.25
Unused, remaining time can NOT be transfered to another customer or to a return visit. Each session is charged as a seperate transaction.
